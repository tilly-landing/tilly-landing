Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies?
+

Yes. We arrive fully equipped with hotel-grade, allergy-tested products and professional tools. You don't need to provide anything — except access to your home.

Are your team members insured and background-checked?
+

Absolutely. Every Tilly specialist is thoroughly vetted, background-checked, and fully insured. We also carry liability insurance for your complete peace of mind.

What is your cancellation policy?
+

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled service. Within 24 hours, we charge 50% of the booking fee to compensate our specialists for reserved time.

How is the 1.5 JOD/m² rate calculated?
+

Simple: total square meters × 1.5 JOD. A 150m² apartment = 225 JOD. No hidden fees. Regular plans (weekly/bi-weekly) include discounts of 10% and 7% respectively.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?
+

Contact us within 48 hours and we'll re-clean the affected areas at no charge. No questions, no hassle. Your satisfaction is the only metric that matters.

Do you serve areas outside Amman?
+

Currently, we focus exclusively on Amman to maintain our quality standards. This includes all neighborhoods from Abdoun and Dabouq to Khalda and the Airport Road corridor.
